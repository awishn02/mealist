Create a profile and add your preferences for information like budget, allergies, desired cooking time and many others! Mealist will then cater each week's menu based on these preferences.
Every week you will get a new shopping list with a list of ingredients for that week's menu. Keep track of your inventory and Mealist will only add items that you need.
If you are concerned with health then Mealist will only select recipes that are nutritious!
